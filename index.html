DEE - Diploma in Electrical Engineering

DEE is a comprehensive 3-year program equipping students with foundational and advanced knowledge in electrical systems. Curriculum includes basic electricity, electrical machines, power distribution, control systems, and safety protocols. Students engage in practical labs with transformers, motors, generators, and wiring simulations. Career prospects: Electrical technician, supervisor in power plants, maintenance engineer, or further studies in B.Tech. The program emphasizes hands-on projects like home wiring models and renewable integrations, preparing for industry demands in manufacturing and utilities.

DCST - Diploma in Computer Science Technology

DCST offers 3-year diploma in computing, covering programming (C, Python), data structures, database management, web development, and networking. Practical training includes software development, hardware assembly, cybersecurity basics, and IoT projects. Labs feature modern PCs for coding and simulations. Graduates pursue roles as junior developers, network admins, IT support, or app developers. Integrates electrical aspects like embedded systems for interdisciplinary skills.

DRE - Diploma in Renewable Energy

DRE is a specialized 3-year diploma focusing on sustainable energy: solar PV systems, wind turbines, bioenergy, energy storage, and smart grids. Coursework includes electrical principles applied to renewables, installation techniques, maintenance, and policy awareness. Hands-on with solar panel setups, inverters, and efficiency testing. Careers: Renewable technician, site supervisor, energy auditor, or roles in green energy firms. Aligns with global shift to clean energy.
